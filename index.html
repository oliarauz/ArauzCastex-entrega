"To express yourself you need a reason, but expressing yourself is the big reason.“ Ai Weiwei

My name is Olivia Arauz Castex and I love to paint. I really enjoy launching myself to create a work that is born from my most authentic version. I have studied in lots of art schools, but my greatest teacher was the experience and the hours in the studio going through the adventure of painting what I feel. I hope you enjoy the tour.
